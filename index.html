About site

FOOD is a creatively designed and generically named web application to help indecisive people like YOU find out what to cook.

1. Simply enter an ingredient and get a random recipe, list of ingredients, and picture of what you’ll be cooking!
2. Scroll to the bottom of the page to view a graph containing maconutrient ratios for a given recipe.
3. Click the name of the recipe to be brought to the recipe site for step by step instructions.

Use us as a jumping off point, or as your primary recipe resource, and find out what’s for dinner!
